My Experience

Reverse Indexing

As an research assistant for the Family History Technology Lab, I made the Reverse Indexing application 12 times more efficient. Then realizing the output from Reverse Indexing was often erroneous, I built a pipeline from the existing application to a new iOS and Android mobile app used to clean up the errors. These features support over ten thousand users as they electronically archive millions of records every year.

Brown Robotics

After placing fourth in an national team robotics competition, I marketed a week long summer robotics class for youth. I developed a curriculum and taught 10 teenagers the basics of programming, engineering, and robotics. After the first week of classes, both the youth and their parents requested a second session, which I then provided.

Ragnar Rankings

I competed in a relay race called the Ragnar. Although the team rankings released included individual times, an easy way to learn how well you compared individually against other racers was not provided. I developed a Python program which read the contents of Ragnar's website and sorted the racers individually, revealing my actual placement in the race.
